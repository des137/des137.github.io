About

I am a Computational Scientist at IBM. In my day-to-day job I analyze and build machine learning models on millions and billions of data points. Before beginning my career as a Data Scientist, I completed my Ph.D. in particle physics from The Graduate Center, CUNY in New York. During my Ph.D. years I applied the theory of Quantum Chromodynamics to investigate the magnetic properties of short-lived particles, called baryons.
